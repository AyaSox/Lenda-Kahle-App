## Part 1: Create a Test Loan Application

### Step 1: Login as Borrower
```
URL: http://localhost:5173/login
Email: borrower@lendakahle.co.za
Password: Borrower@123!
```

**What you'll see:**
- Login page with email and password fields
- Click "LOGIN" button

### Step 2: Apply for a Loan
```
1. Click "MY LOANS" in top navigation
2. Click "Apply for Loan" button (usually a blue button)
3. Fill out the form:
   - Principal Amount: R50000
   - Term: 12 months
   - Purpose: "Business expansion"
4. Click "SUBMIT APPLICATION"
```

**What you'll see:**
- Success message
- Redirected to your loans list
- New loan with **"PENDING"** status (orange badge)

### Step 3: Logout
```
Click "LOGOUT" in top right corner
```
